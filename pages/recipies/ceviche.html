Ceviche

Description

Ceviche is made of diced bits of raw seafood, marinated in a lemon or lime juice concoction. The acid in the citrus 'cooks' the fish until it becomes opaque and soaks in the flavor.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Cucumber
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Fresh Herbs
  • Lime
  • Seasonings
  • Jalapenos
  • Tomato Juice
  • Tortilla Chips

Steps

  1. Dice ingredients
  2. Combine
  3. Let sit in Fridge
