Vegan Potato Curry

Description

A dish composed with a sauce or gravy seasoned with a mixture of ground spices

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Onion
  • Ginger Root
  • Fresh Herbs
  • Chickpeas
  • Seasonings
  • Tomatoes
  • Coconut Milk

Steps

  1. Boil Potatoes
  2. Soften then season aromatics
  3. Combine ingredients and simmer
